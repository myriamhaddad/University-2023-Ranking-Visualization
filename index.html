University 2023 Global Ranking Visualization

This project aims to provide a visualization of informations about universities in a worldwide scale based on data aired in 2023. You can therefore explore opportunities for higher education looking at factors like international exposure, faculty support, and gender representation.
Click on a region to display its top 20 Universities. From there, you are provided comparison plots of 3 features you can choose in the selection menu. Click on a displayed university name to take a look at its collected data.
